What is Innovation Lab?

Innovation Lab is a cutting-edge collaborative environment designed to ignite students' creativity and problem-solving skills. Here, students actively engage with diverse tools and materials, transforming innovative ideas into real-world products. This space not only emphasizes hands-on learning but also cultivates technological proficiency and entrepreneurial thinking. By fostering a culture of experimentation and teamwork, the Innovation Lab nurtures a spirit of curiosity and drives a passion for continuous learning and discovery.

Blended Teaching and Learning Methodology to foster Innovation

  • Instructor-Led Coaching: Our experienced STEM facilitators provide hands-on instruction in the CoE lab at your school throughout the academic year.
  • Age-Appropriate Kits: We design and use our own kits for advanced projects in Robotics, IoT, Embedded Systems, etc.
  • Skill Development: Promotes innovation, creativity, critical thinking, problem-solving, logical thinking, self-management, and teamwork among students.
  • Emerging Technologies: Initiatives to help students acquire knowledge and skills in Robotics, IoT, AI, and Coding.
         Prepares students for future challenges and nation-building as innovators, problem solvers, and entrepreneurs.
  • Curriculum Integration: Robotics, Coding, and AI are incorporated into the school curriculum.
         Coding builds computational thinking, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.
  • Practical Application: Concepts taught in class are reinforced through practical STEM activities.
  • Engaging Curriculum: Integrated with innovative and fascinating practical sessions, making learning interesting and engaging for students.
